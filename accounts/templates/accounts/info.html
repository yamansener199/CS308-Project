We announce the demand for blood to millions of people in seconds!

We know that even seconds are important in cases that require blood supply. For this reason, we invite you to use First Blood.

First blood allows users to post notifications at the time of an accident and other users to contact volunteers via easy-to-use interface.

Step1

When a patient wants to submit a request, patient must get ID from the corresponding doctor. After getting the ID, the patient is enabled to submit the request by filling other related informations.

Step2

The generated form becomes visible to everyone on the First blood dashboard. After the request is delivered by donater, the request will be closed and deleted by the corresponding doctor.
